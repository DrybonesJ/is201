Alabard 2 Coming Soon!

Title TBD

Posted by Justin on December 7, 2022

We have now hit the 3/4 mark on writing Alabard 2!

I've just finished the sequence that finished act 2 and starts to move into act 3. Things are moving along at a good pace, thanks to NaNoWriMo, and hopefully we'll be able to finisht the majority of the book by the end of the year, especially with the extended winter break that I'll have.

As far as a second draft, there are only a few minor details that I'll want to fix before giving it to my alpha readers to see what they have to say about it. I imagine that after that, I'll have to do some major, nitpicking work in order to get everything in a sequence where it builds things up write and leads to a satisfying ending.

As I've mentioned in the past, this book will continue to follow Alabard, starting with him as he ascends the throne in Crayal. But I've also introduced two other main characters named Tori and Elron who are opposing Alabard as he begins a war on Koralism. It's a bit different, and probably not what was expected, but I hope it's enjoyable to read.

After I finish this novel, I'll move on from Gordal, trying my hand at a new book that I've recently been plotting in my head. It'll be titled The Sacred Silence. More details about it will come soon.
